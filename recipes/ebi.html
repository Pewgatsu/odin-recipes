Ebi Fry

These deep-fried shrimp are coated with crispy panko crumbs to make the easiest, tastiest deep-fried shrimp ever!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb prawn
  • salt
  • black Pepper
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups panko (Japanese breadcrumbs)
  • 4 cups oil

Tartar Sauce

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 pickle
  • salt
  • black Pepper
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 4 tbsp japanese mayonnaise

Steps:

To create the tartar sauce:

  1. Boil the Eggs. Make sure to cover the eggs with water. Bring the water to a boil and rotate the eggs with chopsticks or spoon.
  2. Dice the onions and soak it in cold water for 10 mins.
  3. Finely chop the pickles.
  4. Drain the onion after 10 mins.
  5. In a bowl, break the eggs into small pieces using a fork.
  6. Add the pickles, onions, salt, and pepper then squeeze some lemon juice.
  7. Finally, add the mayonnaise and mix all together. Cover and set aside in the fridge.

To create the Ebi Fry

  1. Season the prawn with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge the prawns with flour, and then beaten egg. Double dredge with flour and egg and then dredge in panko.
  3. Shake off the excess.
  4. Heat oil to 180 degrees celcius or 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  5. Put 2-3 prawns and deep fry them for about 2 and a half minutes or until golden brown. Transfer to a wire rack or paper towel to dry off excess oil.
  6. Between batches, make sure to scoop up breadcrumbs to keep the oil clean.
